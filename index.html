clipfactory

Site: https://aizenscode.github.io/clipfactory-site/

A personal command-line pipeline that turns my long-form videos into vertical short clips with captions, and uploads them to my own YouTube channel, @surfs_tories.

clipfactory uses YouTube API Services to upload the operator's own original videos to the operator's own YouTube channel. It is a private, single-user tool: it is not distributed, has no other users, and collects no data from anyone but its operator.

By using this application, the operator agrees to the YouTube Terms of Service.
